About D&Y Fancy Collections

"Your Family's Fashion Destination"

At D&Y Fancy Collections, we believe fashionable clothing should be accessible to everyone in the family. Located in Nangal Raya, Delhi Cantt, we've been serving our community with quality apparel since 2010.

Our unique pricing model ensures that whether you're shopping for your toddler, teenager, or yourself, you'll find stylish options at ₹500 to ₹1500. We carefully curate our collections to offer the best value without compromising on quality.

Our Mission

"Your Family's Fashion Destination"

At D & Y Collection, our mission is to make fashion accessible, stylish, and affordable for everyone — from kids to women and men. We are committed to delivering high-quality garments that blend comfort with the latest trends, ensuring that our customers feel confident in what they wear.

Along with serving individual shoppers, we also focus on wholesale supply, supporting retailers and resellers with a wide range of ready-made garments at competitive prices.

Our goal is to become a trusted name in everyday and occasion wear, known for reliability, variety, and value, while continuously evolving with the needs of modern fashion.

Our Vision

"Your Family's Fashion Destination"

Our vision is to become a leading name in the fashion industry, recognized for bringing together style, quality, and affordability under one roof. We aspire to make D & Y Collection (Dandy) a go-to destination for kids, women, and men’s garments, while also empowering wholesale partners and retailers with reliable and trend-forward collections.

We aim to expand our presence nationally and globally, building a community where fashion is not just about clothing, but about confidence, identity, and expression. By embracing innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, we see Dandy as a brand that inspires trust, celebrates diversity, and sets new standards in ready-made fashion.
